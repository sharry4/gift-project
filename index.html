I always struggle with what to give as gifts during various occasions to different people. Duirng those times, Internet and Google are always my friend in helping with ideas. Out of my own struggle, I wondered how many people around the world need the same kind of help. In this project, I looked at the various thing people search for related to gifts.

Gifts Search Interest

Across the globe, Christmas as the most celebrated holiday/occasion, its gift search has the highest search interest across years is not that much of a surprise. After Christmas gift search are birthday gifts and anniversary gifts. Birthday as a common occasion for celebration, gift

Birthday Gifts

One interesting thing emerged from search data is that across years, there seem to always have slightly more searches for birthday during the month of July, August and September. And a huge drop during the month of November and December.

Gifts Search in US

Across US, Christmas gift are most searched in West Virginia, followed by South and North Dakoda.

Searches Across the World

Interesting enough, the Falkland Island has the most search across the world for Christmas.Falkland Island also has the most search for "gifts for her" and "gifts for him".
