Coming up with gift ideas during various occasions for different people has been a constant struggle for me. And Google are my dreast friend in those times. Out of curiosity, I wondered how many people are facing the same kind of problem. After some digging, I am relieved to know that I am not alone!

What does people search gifts for?

It is not suprising that Christmas turns out to be the most popular holiday/occasion to send gifts for. The gift search term for Christmas has the highest search interest across all years around the globe. After Christmas gift search are birthday gifts and anniversary gifts. Birthday gifts search
*Each line in the graphs represents a year and the y-axis represents month.

One interesting thing emerged from search data is that across years, there seem to always have slightly more searches for birthday during the month of July, August and September. And a huge drop during the month of November and December.
Gifts search for mom
Mom as a popular subject for gifts search holds an interetsing pattern. The slight peak in May is probably the result of Mother's day but the huge peak in December seems to indicate mom is also a popular gift receiver during the time of Christmas.
Gifts search for dad
Interestingly enough, gifts search for dad follows a similar pattern as the search for mom did. Despite the slight peak in June, which possible caused by Father's day, the huge peak came in December. Both side of the parents seems to receive gifts a lot during the month of Decemeber.
Gifts search for boyfriend
The gifts search pattern for boyfriend is also very similar to the search pattern for mom and dad did. With a smaller peak in the month of Feburary probably due to Valentine's Day, the search term reaches its max on December as well.
Gift search for girlfriend
Same pattern shows up for the gifts search for girlfriend as the search pattern for boyfriend did—a small peak in Feburary with a huge peak in December. December seem to be a huge month for gifts maybe due to Christmas and New Year.
Gift search for teachers
A seaonal pattern corresponds to the graduation time can also be found on gift searches for teachers. Two clear peaks can be found during May and December.

What does people in US search for?

I took a closer look at some of the popular gift search terms across the United States on a state level, and it seems like people in New York, pennsylvania, Wyoming, South and North Dakoda need a lot more help on choosing gifts in general.

What about the world?

Across all popular search terms, United States, Canada and Australia tend to have a higher search rate compare to rest of the world. Among all search terms, Christmas gift and birthday gift search seem to be the more popular terms compare to others.
